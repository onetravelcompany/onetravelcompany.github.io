Istanbul City Tour

Our itinerary begins with the Hippodrome, an ancient venue for chariot races, featuring the Obelisk of Theodosius, the bronze Serpentine Column, and the Column of Constantine. Next, we'll explore the Sultanahmet Imperial Mosque, also known as the Blue Mosque for its stunning blue Iznik tiles. Then, we'll visit the Hagia Sophia Grand Mosque, a remarkable example of Byzantine Architecture and formerly known as the Church of Divine Wisdom..

BURSA FULL DAY CITY TOUR WITH ULUDAG MOUNTAINS WITH TELEFERIK

Explore Bursa with a ferry trip crossing Yalova, immersing yourself in its rich history and culture. Must-visit locations includ:

Bursa offers diverse attractions such as:

  • Bursa Grand Mosque: A stunning example of Ottoman architecture and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Bursa Castle: Explore the ancient castle for panoramic city views.
  • Historic Bazaars: Wander through the Grand Bazaar and Silk Market for souvenirs.
  • Bursa City Museum: Discover the city's history and culture through exhibits.
  • Bursa Zoo and Botanical Park: Ideal for families seeking a day in nature.
  • Tomb of Osman Gazi: Visit the final resting place of the Ottoman Empire's founder.
  • Bursa Cable Car: Ride to Mount Uludağ for scenic views and outdoor activities.
  • Cumalıkızık Village: Experience a charming Ottoman-era village.
  • Bursa Atatürk Museum: Explore the former residence of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk.
  • Bursa Thermal Baths: Relax in therapeutic thermal baths like Çekirge and Oylat.
PRINCESS ISLAND FULL DAY TOUR

It's one of Istanbul's top sightseeing spots, drawing both domestic and international tourists. Enjoy the peaceful, car-free environment as you explore by electric car. Start with a ferry trip, followed by a lunch featuring fresh, seasonal specialties. Enjoy free time to swim, shop for souvenirs, or admire Ottoman mansions.
